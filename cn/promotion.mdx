## Rules

- Each Organization ID can participate once during the campaign period and receive one voucher reward.
- If multiple top-ups are made during the campaign period, the system will automatically identify the single transaction with the highest value among all top-ups made during the campaign period, specifically the highest-value transaction occurring on the day of the user's first top-up in the period. The reward will be calculated proportionally based on this specific transaction, while all other top-ups will not qualify for rewards.
- Eligibility is based on the actual time the Kimi Open Platform receives the funds.
- Eligibility for this campaign is calculated independently and is unrelated to any previous top-up reward promotions.
- Top-up amounts are calculated in USD and rounded down to the nearest whole dollar.
- Vouchers are issued within 1 business day after recharge.
- The maximum voucher amount is $4,000.
- Vouchers can be used for all purchases on the Kimi platform.
- Vouchers are valid for **90 days** and are automatically cleared after expiration.
- Voucher balance is deducted before cash balance when applicable.
- Recharge amounts are **non-refundable**.
